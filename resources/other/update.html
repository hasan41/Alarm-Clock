Please follow the simple steps to update your Alarm clock

  1. The updates are NOT provided by the Ubuntu Software Center.
  2. Please connect your pc to internet before proceeding.
  3. The updator will ask you for your computer password to update. This is required to update the binaries of Alarm clock as they are placed in the root folder of your system.
  4. After the update is complete, please restart the Alarm clock to see the effects.
